About Me

keywords: designer, developer, disciple

So a little about me.

I love music, cartoons, working with kids, and Jesus.

But professionally, I love production. I love making things work. I love producing videos and graphics. I love getting to be creative with my work. I love using the Adobe creative suite to give life to my ideas. Animation is a huge part of that, and I so desire to gain more skills in that area.

I came to Georgia Tech in the fall of 2013 wanting to get smarter. What I ended up learning was that what I really wanted was to get more creative. Through the wild ride of switching majors as a yearly tradition, I ended up finding Computational Media. This major seeks to bridge the technical and creative aspects of design, which parallels my own interests.

My goal for the near future is to graduate from Georgia Tech with a degree in Computational Media and skills in production and combine the two into a career.
